About Us

myFuel is your ultimate trip planner. Planning for a trip has never been easier. Simply put in your car model, the distance of your trip, and that is it. You will get the full amount of fuel you will need together with the price according to our Kenyan fuel retail prices. Calculating manually seems a little daunting hence we make life eaiser for you.
Now go on, lets get driving.
